Who are we?

As the name suggests, Fundraiser believes in bringing a smile on faces that deserve it the most but do not have access to happiness in their lives. We aim to create a sustainable impact in the lives of the children at-risk by developing strong programs focussed on their upbringing and surrounding. We feel that factors like improper upbringing and poor mental health affects the minds of these young kids at a very primary stage in their childhood, resulting in devastating effects like missing out on education, high risk of addiction and abuse, and stunted personal growth. Our main aim, as an organisation, is to expose our children to the skills of personality building and 21st century life skills that are ignored in schools.
